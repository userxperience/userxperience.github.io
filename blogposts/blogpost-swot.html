my SWOT-analysis

I have made a SWOT(Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, Threat) analysis about myself.

Strength: what do you do best? What are your positive traits?

A: I'm good at working individually and independently.


Weakness: what personality traits may be holding you back in your studies / career?

A: I'm bad at learning things by heart, especially without proper reason.


Opportunities: how can you turn your strengths / weaknesses into opportunities?

A: by picking a study that's practical and studying hard when I need to.


Threats: what obstacles do you face to succeed as a student? Could any of your weaknesses prevent you from succeeding in your studies / career?

A: deadlines that are far away may be a threat as I (like most students) sometimes tend to procrastinate.
