the first company I signed up for was YourSurprise, a company offering a service of sending personalized gifts.

The second company was a security firm named VeraCode, but the presentation had a lot of technical jargon I wasn't familiar with yet.
After the presentations, we were divided into groups and were instructed to create posters about various websites. Our company was Atos.
